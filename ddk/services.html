Visa Pathway

E-2 Treaty Investors

Citizens of certain countries in which the United States has an agreement are eligible for an E-2 visa. The E-2, which is temporary and must be renewed regularly, will permit one to start a business with some capital investment.

Permanent Residence

EB-5 Immigrant Investors

For those individuals seeking permanent residence in the United States ("Green Card"), with access to a substantial amount of capital and the creation of ten full time jobs, this is the best method.

Executive Mobility

L-1 Intracompany Transfers

For companies with a presence in the United States (or are planning to establish one), the L-1 visa will allow you to transfer executives and key personnel from one company location to another.
